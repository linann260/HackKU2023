Our Mission

Issue:

According to EPA.gov, approximately one third of all food in the United States goes uneaten. In 2019 EPA estimated that 96 percent of households' wasted food ended up in landfills, combustion facilties, or down the drain to the sewer system.

Why was Souper created?:

Souper was created to reduce the amount of food waste occuring by providing an easy way for users to search for new recipes based on one ingredient. Souper introduces users to a new way they can use their ingredient. Therefore helping with food waste reduction.

Mission:

Preventing food from going to waste is one of the easiest ways to lower our climate change footprint as well as saving users money.
